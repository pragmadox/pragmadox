Intro

Jay Price is a veteran of the United States Marine Corps where he worked as an Arabic Linguist and Intelligence Analyst supporting missions in the Middle East and Africa. In 2014, He completed his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and Asian Languages and Civilizations, with certificates in Middle East Studies and International Relations .

After interning with the House of Representatives and Stroz Friedberg, he completed his Master of Science in Data Analytics at Fordham University in New York in 2017.

His interests include unmanned aerial vehicles, photography, running, skydiving, web development, and data analysis.
